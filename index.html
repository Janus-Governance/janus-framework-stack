Janus Governance Core

Computable Governance for AI-Assisted Systems

Janus is a governance model that records not only what happened, but also what was required to happen and did not occur.

Core Principles

  • Evidence model (E+ / E−)
  • Omission as structured evidence
  • Deterministic rebuild from logs
